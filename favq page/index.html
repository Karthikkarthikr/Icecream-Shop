WHAT IS THE ICE CREAM SHOP?

The Ice Cream Shop is a virtual restaurant that delivers America’s favorite ice cream brands straight to your door!

WHAT BRANDS DO YOU SELL?

We sell Unilever’s most beloved ice cream brands: Ben & Jerry’s, Talenti, Breyers, Magnum Ice Cream, Popsicle, Klondike, and Good Humor. Availability may vary.

CAN I PICK UP THE ICE CREAM IN STORE?

The Ice Cream Shop is delivery only. Of course, you can find these brands in most major grocery and convenience stores nationwide.

WILL MY ICE CREAM MELT BEFORE IT ARRIVES?

Not on our watch! We work with our delivery partners to ensure the ice cream gets from our freezers to your home quickly, usually in 30 minutes or less.
